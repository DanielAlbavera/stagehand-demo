# Workshop Task: Integrating Stagehand into Early Playwright Builds
 
## Steps
 
1. **Navigate to amazon.com**
   - Open your browser and go to [amazon.com](https://www.amazon.com).
 
2. **Enter a search term**
   - Fill the search term (e.g., `smartwatch`) in the searchbar
   - Click the search button.
 
3. **Observe and extract product titles and prices**
   - Review the list of products displayed in the search results.
   - For each product, identify and record the product title and price.
   - Make sure to extract this information for multiple products to demonstrate data collection.

4. **Validate search term in product title**
   - Check that the search term you entered appears in at least one of the product titles.
   - This ensures the search functionality is working as expected and relevant results are being shown.
 
5. **Add at least one product to shopping cart**
   - Choose at least one product from the search results.
   - Click on the product to view its details, then select the option to add it to your shopping cart.
   - Confirm that the product has been successfully added.
 
6. **Navigate to shopping cart and validate products**
   - Go to your shopping cart by clicking the cart icon or link.
   - Verify that all products you added are present in the cart.
   - Check that the product titles and prices match what you previously extracted.
